Ophiocordyceps unilateralis fungus:

The fungus “Ophiocordyceps unilateralis fungus” is commonly known as the zombie-ant fungus (Wikipedia, 2020). This fungus uses ants as hosts typically is typically found in tropical forests (Lu, 2019). It infects the ant by penetrating the exoskeleton and slowly growing inside the ant and taking control over its behavior. Once in full control, the fungus moves the ant into a warm and humid location near the ground, ideal for distributing its spores (Petruzzello, 2023). The fungus will cause the ant to climb up a nearby twig or stem and lock onto it with its jaws (Science Section, Library of Congress). Soon after death follows, the fungus finishes digesting the cadaver before its final stage. For the final stage of the fungus cycle, it erects a stalk from the base of the ant's head that will release spores to infect other ants (Science Section, Library of Congress).

The fungus does not directly control the ants brain instead it uses bioactive compounds that interact directly with the ant’s nervous system and directly control the ants muscles, (Lu, 2019). The fungus also differs from typical zombie lore as it does not typically infect and entire colony to keep the ecosystem balanced the fungi only infect a few ants at any given time, (Lu, 2019).

The fungi also seems to have a good level of control on where the anys dye as graveyards of dozens of ants infected with ophiocordyceps unilateralis have been found together. This suggest just how precisely the fungus can control the death of the infected, (Petruzzello).

In a very dystopian twice, Sam Altman, the creator of OpenAI, is often seen walking around with a blue backpack that carries his laptop that contains the power to shut down Chat-GPT if it gets out of control the same way Skynet did (Arpit Dubey, 2023).

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an action role-playing game from CD Projekt/CD Projekt Red. In which you play as V, a mercenary who gets implanted with a cybernetic “bio-chip” which contains an engram of the rock star Johnny Silverhand (Wikipedia Contributors, 2019). Johnny Silverhand is voiced by Keeanu Reeves (Behind the Voice Actors, 2025).

Plot: During the prologue, the main character V with contracted along with Jackie Welles to steal the chip of immortality from the Arasaka Corporation. They are contracted by the character Dexter Deshawn. The two of them manage the braking into the megacorp and steal the device, but the plan goes off the rails when the character Jackie appears to die. After meeting backup with Dexter V is betrayed and left to die in a landfill, but they manage to survive in part due to the chip of immortality (Cyberpunk Fandom Wiki, n.d.).

At the start of the game, there are 3 options to play as: The Momad, The Streetkid, The Corpo-Rat. (Toms, 2020)

Nomad Lifepath: The momad life path with the polar opposite of Corpo. It starts with the player, V, starting in the Badlands outside of Night City. This path allows you to learn about the clans that roam the far reaches of the Cuberpunk 2077. At the start, you will have to make a major life decision: whether to ditch the clan you've been roaming with and drive into Night City. During the drive, your car breaks down and you will have to enlist the age the help of a familiar face to help repair your car (Toms, 2020).

Street Kid: As a street Kid, you will get placed again in Night City from the start, but this time you will start on the ground rather than in one of the corpo-controlled skyscrapers. Down in the group, everyone knows everyone else, and work comes in the form of favours and trading, along with solving problems for one another. Your first quest as a street kid is to help a local bartender settle his debt with a local fixer. But to do this, V needs to steal a car from an Araka corporation (Toms, 2020).

Corpo: If you pick the Corpo life path, you start at the highest echelons of Night City from the moment you start. You work for Arasaka, working under a man called Arthur Jenkins. Your first quest involves helping Jenkins take down a rival inside the corporation.

Technology: Cyberpunk 2077 was developed using CD Projekt Red’s internal game engine REDengine 4. This is the fourth iteration of this engine, in which it implemented various lighting adjustments to create a much more realistic world, including hardware-accelerated real-time ray tracing. This allows for much more realistic lighting and making their games much more realistic.
