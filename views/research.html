Ophiocordyceps unilateralis fungus

This fungus infects ants and other insects and controls their movements, its end goal is to self-propagate and disperse (Lu, 2019).
I would like to use this fungus as inspiration for how the AI would spread its control across the population. The fungus cause the ants to leave their canopy nests for the forst floor which is more suitable for the fungal growth (Wikipedia Ophiocordyceps unilateralis, 2020).
I would use a similar consecpt for my AI to spread its control across the population. Bringing them to one central location where the AI would have more control due to the proximity to the tower.
